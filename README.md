**Fish Released From Planes to Restock Utah Lakes**\
`The Utah Division of Wildlife Resources said that last week it used planes to stock about 200 lakes inaccessible by vehicle. The division said it has aerial-stocked fish since the 1950s and that they have a high survival rate.`\
